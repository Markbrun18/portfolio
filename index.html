About

Helping you tell your story

With nearly 10 years of experience in the creative industry, I began my career in jewelry photo retouching before earning a BS in Graphics and Design. In the last four years, I have specialized in wedding post-production and video editing, crafting polished wedding films, highlights, and visual content that capture genuine emotions and memorable moments. Passionate about storytelling and attention to detail, I strive to deliver high-quality work that brings every client's vision to life.
